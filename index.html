Welcome to First Baptist Church Itire

First Baptist Church is a vibrant and inclusive community of faith, dedicated to spreading the teachings of Jesus Christ and serving others. With a strong foundation in Baptist traditions, First Baptist Church emphasizes the importance of biblical truth, authentic worship, and compassionate service. The church's mission is to empower hearts, enrich lives, and extend God's love to all, regardless of background or circumstance.
